Back to the Future!

Back to the Future is a movie trilogy about time travel! It is my favorite series and I'm going to educate you on it through my perspective!

For the scope of this project, I have decided to exclude the third movie in the series as it is my least favorite, and I'd rather focus my attention on the first two movies.
